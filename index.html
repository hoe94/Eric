About

Eric Low is a native of Ampang, Selangor. He's born in Ampang. He has moved to Seri Kembangan with his family since 8 years old. The most important decision in his life he has made, which is joined SJKC Serdang Baru 1 School Band. Eric started his musical journey during primary school and chosen euphonium as his instrument. After that, He has continue pursuit his interest in SMK Seri Kembangan Wind Band during his high school life.

Furthermore, he had continue pursuit his musical journey during his university life. He had realized that music is essential of live. Currently, he is the active player of SKY Wind Band and KLPAC SB.
